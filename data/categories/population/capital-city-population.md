Capital city populationOver or under specified population of capital city. Only the official capital area is considered.

The data for each city's population is taken from the information box of that city's Wikipedia page. The data is retrieved from the Wikipedia pages every 1st of each month.

Note on countries with multiple capitals:For Capital city population, the category is true if the population of any of the official capitals matches the specified number.
